Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our workplace language training programs

How long does training take?

We offer three flexible engagement options to meet your organisation's needs:

  • • 12-week trial: Perfect for testing the program with a small group
  • • 6-month subscription: Ideal for seeing measurable results
  • • 1-year subscription: Best value for long-term transformation

All programs include weekly workshops, 24/7 web platform access, cultural audit reports, performance reports and more. Details coming soon!

What makes Vocare different from other English training programs?

We focus specifically on workplace communication in the care sector. Our training combines real workplace scenarios with cultural fluency, not just language skills. Plus, our AI-powered feedback provides personalized coaching that adapts to each employee's progress and workplace challenges.

How is training delivered?

Only in person workshops and digital platform access. We know that language learning needs to be face to face for best results. Our hybrid solution matches the need for flexibility, accountability and real results.

How do you measure the effectiveness of the training?

We track multiple metrics including communication confidence scores, workplace incident reduction, employee feedback, and retention rates. Our AI platform provides detailed analytics on individual and team progress, and we deliver regular reports showing ROI and improvement trends.

What if some employees are hesitant to participate?

We understand that participating in language training can feel vulnerable. Our approach is supportive and non-judgmental, focusing on practical skills that immediately improve workplace confidence. We build a strong learning community where participants support each other, offer monthly gatherings to practice in a relaxed setting, and provide rewards and recognition for participation and progress. We work with you to create a positive culture around learning and show clear career benefits for participants. Many find that the supportive community aspect becomes one of their favourite parts of the program.

Do you provide training for specific roles or departments?

Absolutely! We customise training for specific roles like care workers, nurses, support coordinators, and supervisors. Each program includes role-specific vocabulary, communication scenarios, and workplace protocols relevant to that position within your organisation.

What's included in the free consultation?

During our consultation, we'll assess your team's specific communication needs, discuss your organisational goals, review current challenges, and provide a customised training proposal. We'll also demonstrate our AI platform and show you exactly how we measure success. No obligations, just valuable insights for your team.

How do you measure linguistic clarity improvement specifically?

We go beyond general language levels like B1 or B2. Instead, we track specific, measurable improvements in workplace communication. This includes clarity scores for critical workplace vocabulary, pronunciation accuracy for safety-critical terms, response time in emergency scenarios, and correct usage of specific technical phrases relevant to your industry. Each learner receives detailed benchmarks showing their progress on the exact words and phrases they use daily, not just general language proficiency scores.
